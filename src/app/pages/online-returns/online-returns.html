Returns & Shipping Refund Policy

We are committed to providing a transparent shopping experience. Please review our specific policies regarding product returns and shipping fee refunds below.

No Product Refunds

Please note that POP MART does not offer refunds for product purchases once the order is finalized.

Shipping Refunds

Shipping fees are only refundable in the case of a valid and approved order cancellation.

Return Eligibility

Returns for exchange are only accepted for items in original packaging with seals intact, or manufacturing defects.

Return Process

1

Submit Request

Go to Order Detail, select a Completed order, and choose 'Return'. Provide reason and photo evidence.

2

System Check

The system verifies eligibility, order status, and evidence. Invalid requests are Rejected.

3

Admin Review

Valid requests are set to Pending. Admin reviews and decides Approved or Rejected.

4

Shipping & Completion

If approved, a return shipment is created. Once the carrier confirms, status becomes Completed.

Eligibility Standards

To ensure fairness for all collectors, returns for exchange must meet these criteria:

  • Intact Seals Blind Boxes and limited editions must remain sealed and unused for return eligibility.
  • Manufacturing Defects Issues like paint defects, broken parts, or missing accessories from the factory will be prioritized.
  • Proof of Purchase A valid order ID or receipt from official POP MART stores is required.

Important Note

Promotional items, gifts, and opened Blind Boxes (not due to manufacturing defects) cannot be returned to maintain the surprise element of the product.

Shipping Refund Timelines (Cancellations Only)

For valid order cancellations where a shipping fee refund is applicable, the following timeline applies:

3-5 Business Days PROCESSING TIME
7-10 Days BANK TRANSFER
